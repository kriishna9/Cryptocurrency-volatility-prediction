What is this Project?

This is an AI-powered tool designed to predict cryptocurrency Volatility.

What is Volatility?

Volatility measures how rapidly a cryptocurrency's price changes. High volatility means the price swings wildly (higher risk), while low volatility means the price is relatively stable.

How it Works (Machine Learning)

We use a Machine Learning algorithm to analyze real-time market data (like open/close prices, volume, and market cap) to predict future risk levels automatically.
